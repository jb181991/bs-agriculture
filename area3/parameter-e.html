AREA III - PARAMETER E

Graduation Requirements

  • S.1. The required number of faculty (16 full time permanent) of faculty members have earned graduate degrees appropriate and relevant to the Agriculture program (100% of full time; 50% of part time), or any of the allied fields of agricultural engineering, agribusiness, agroforestry, animal science and animal husbandry (CMO No. 14, S. 2008).
    • S.1.1. The agriculture program with specialization has a minimum of 75% of the faculty with graduate degrees in the discipline or its equivalent. For others, without specialization, a minimum of 50% have at least a master’s degree in the discipline or its equivalent (CMO No. 14, S. 2008); and
    • S.1.2. Those teaching the professional courses have valid PRC certificate of registration or professional agriculturist’s license (PRC Law).
  • S.2. Other qualification such as the following are considered:
    • S.2.1 professional related experience;
    • S.2.2 licensure;
    • S.2.3 technical skills and competence;
    • S.2.4 specialization; and
    • S.2.5 special abilities, computer literacy, research capability.
  • S.3 Faculty handling OJT/practicum courses have had at least three (3) years of teaching and industry-based experience in the field.
  • S.4 At least 50% of the faculty have relevant graduate degrees.

  • I.1 The faculty demonstrate professional competence and performed in any or a combination of the following:
    • I.1.1 instruction;
    • I.1.2 research
    • I.1.3 extension;
    • I.1.4 production;
    • I.1.5 consultancy and expert service; and
    • I.1.6 publication, creative and scholarly work.
  • I.2 Faculty pursue graduate degrees in Agriculture and/or allied fields from reputable institutions (with at least Level II accreditation status or world/Asian ranked).

  • O.1 The institution has qualified and competent faculty.
