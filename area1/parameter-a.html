AREA I - PARAMETER A

STATEMENT OF VISSION, MISSION, GOALS AND OBJECTIVES

  • S.1. The institution has a system in determining the Vision and Mission.
  • S.2. The Vision clearly reflects what the institution hopes to become in the future
  • S.3. The Mission clearly reflects the institution's legal and other statutory mandate
  • S.4. The Goal of the College/academic Unit are consistent with the Mission of the Institution
  • S.5. The Objectives, based on CMO 14, s. 2008, have the expected outcomes in terms of competencies (skills and knowledge), values and other attributes of the graduates which include the development of:)
    • S.5.1. Scientific habit of thought;
    • S.5.2. Entry-level competencies in technical Agriculture
    • S.5.3. research and extension capabilities;
    • S.5.4. entrepreneurial skills;
    • S.5.5. students' own ideas, desirable attitudes, personal discipline and moral character;
    • S.5.6. critical thinking skills; and
    • S.5.7. aesthetic and cultural values.
  • S.6. The VMGO, based on CMO 14, s.2008, contains the following provisions:
    • S.6.1. equipping students with skills in identifying, diagnosing and analyzing problems;
    • S.6.2. competence in applying technologies needed in the development and conservation of agriculture and food system resource;
    • S.6.3. preparation of students for careers in research, farm, management, agriculture, food technology, entrepreneurship and teaching; and
    • S.6.4. meeting the needs for professional agriculturist;

  • I.1. the institution/College conducts a review on the statement of the Vision and Mission as well as its goals and program objectives for the approval of authorities concerned.
  • I.2. The College/Academic Unit follows a system of formulating its goals and objectives for the approval of authorities concerned.
  • I.3. The College/Academic Unit Faculty, personnel, students and other stakeholders (cooperating agencies, linkages, alumni, industry sector and other concerned groups) participate in the formulation, review and/or revision of the VMGO.

  • O.1. the VMGO, are crafted and duly approved by the BOR/BOT.
